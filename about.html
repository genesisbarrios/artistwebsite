GENESIS BARRIOS

Biography

Genesis Barrios is a multi-faceted artist based in Miami, Florida. She started studying music at the age of seven in school. Genesis is a multi-instrumentalist, singer, and producer. Most of her influences are artists that exist in the Jazz, RnB, and Pop world. Aside from making and performing music, Genesis is an Undergraduate Computer Science student.
